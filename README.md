# Mint Farm

A turn-based farming game. Play it at https://farm-game-coral.vercel.app/

---

## Basics

You get 11 action points (AP) each day. Most actions cost 1 or 0 AP. When you run out or are done for the day, hit Next Day - crops grow, traps fire, passive income comes in, and you get to pick a free reward.

The basic loop: till a plot, plant a seed, water it, harvest when it's ready. 

---

## Crops

| Crop | Grow time | Sell price |
|---|---|---|
| Mint | 1 day | 100g |
| Wheat | 2 days | 250g |
| Cabbage | 3 days | 350g |

Crops only grow if they're watered, either manually (costs 1 AP) or automatically if a Water Tower is adjacent.

---

## Structures

Built in Build mode. Costs vary — some need gold, some need wood or rock (see Resources below).

**Water Tower** — auto-waters all 8 neighboring tiles each day. Essential if you want to scale up planting.

**Growth Tower** — crops in range grow one day faster. Stacks with water.

**Beehive** — 250g per day, no input needed. Put a Keg next to it to double that.

**Fruit Tree** — harvest once per day for 100g. No water required.

**Tea Leaves** — harvest once per day for 350g, but needs a Water Tower adjacent.

**Keg** — doubles the payout from any Beehive, Fruit Tree, or Tea nearby.

**Bird Trap** — each day it picks one ripe crop next to it at random, eats it, and gives you 750g. High reward, some risk.

**Trap Doubler** — makes nearby traps pay out 1125g instead of 750g.

**Plant Doubler** — 1.5x harvest value for crops next to it.

**Action Flower** — takes 4 days to bloom. Click it in harvest mode to fully restore your AP. Works even at 0 AP.

---

## Resources

Rocks and wild trees appear on the map. Use Harvest mode to clear them — you get 1 rock or 1 wood respectively, and the tile becomes usable farmland. Several structures require these to build.

---

## Selling

Switch to Sell mode and click any structure to remove it and recover its full build cost — gold, wood, and rock included. Useful for reorganising your layout.

---

## Daily Reward

Each morning you're offered 3 options — pick one. It's always free to place. Usually a structure or a farmland plot. Worth planning around since some rewards (beehive, water tower) can save you a lot of gold early on.

---

## Milestone

There's a gold target shown at the top. Hit it and it doubles. The game doesn't end — it just keeps scaling.

---

## A few things worth knowing

- Water Tower + Growth Tower together is the fastest crop setup
- Bird Traps are most efficient when surrounded by quick-growing crops like Mint
- Use Action Flowers only at 0 AP to maximize their benefit
- Clear the rocks and trees early, the resources unlock most of the better buildings
